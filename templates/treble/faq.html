Frequently Asked Questions

What is treble?

Treble is a simple way to find new recommendations for songs you might like.

How does it work?

You can add new songs powered by Spotify. You can then recommend songs that are similar to it. You can also review songs.

What is a comment?

A comment is a 256 character text review of a song, as well as a cute reaction emoji. You can only have one review per song.
