Frequently Asked Questions

What is Treble?

Ever found a song you really love, but never manage to find anything else quite like it?
That's where Treble comes in. We provide a platform for music lovers to share their favourite music, recommend songs to other users, and review all kinds of songs!


How does it work?

Simply sign up for your free account, add your favourite songs and you're good to go!
The next time someone adds a recommendation to one of your favourites, it will show up on your account page!


But where do you get the music metadata? Please tell me I don't have to add it all in myself...?

Of course not!
We use the Spotify API to ensure you get all the information about any song you want to add, such as artwork, album, artist and genre, so that you don't have to bother. Just search Spotify for the song you're looking for, hit "Add Song" and we'll add all the information you need to our database!


So how do I add a recommendation or review a song?

If you want to add a recommendation, use our intuitive auto-completing search bar to navigate to the song you're looking for, and add your recommendation. You can either add a song that is already in our database, or search Spotify for your own addition!
If you want to let everyone else know how great (or dissapointing..) a song in our database is, just scroll to the bottom of the page for that song, and add your review, as well as an emoji reaction to summarise it!


Will other people be able to view my profile?

Viewing another user's profile is exclusive to user's who have a Treble account and are logged in. If another user is logged in and wants to view your profile, they can use the search bar, and will be able to see your favourite songs and your reviews.
