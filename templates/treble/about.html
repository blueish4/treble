About Us

We wanted to make Treble because we love music!


However, often we get narrowed down into the songs we already know we like, and struggle to broaden our horizons.
So we came up with the idea of an app that can suggest new music to us, based on what we already like!

We made Treble for our second year Web Application Development course at the University of Glasgow.
